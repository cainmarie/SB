ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Selena!

I am an upcoming graphic artist and animator, and I create visuals that are stylish and compelling. I like to use my art to tell a story because I believe that is where passion and inspiration can really come to life. You give me a message and stylus, and you better believe that something beautiful is about to be created. I started learning about design and art in North Allegheny High School where I participated in the National Art Honors Society. From there, I applied to the Graphic Information Technology program at Arizona State University. Now, I am currently pursuing my BS in Graphic Information Technology.
