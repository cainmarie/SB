PORTFOLIO

Weatherly App Animation

Weatherly is a fictional weather app I created and designed for a UI/UX project. My goal was to create a mockup that would resemble the app's live navigation from the home screen to its secondary screens and menu. I wanted to create a fast-paced navigation with quick transitions and simple layouts. The final GIF was output from After Effects and spans about 10 seconds.

Karmilla E-mail Design

Karmilla Wear is a fictional brand that I created an e-mail advertisement for to practice my typography and design skills. The final product was made in InDesign with Adobe Fonts and free stock photos. All photo rights go to iStock and Adobe Stock.

Digital Combat Simulator Advertisement

This project called for a 3D animated video that would showcase skills like modeling, rigging, and animating. I chose to create an advertisement for the Digital Combat Simulator, a video game with 3D graphics. My final product was a 20-second ad displaying a dogfight between 3D model jets on YouTube that demonstrated the exciting and visually engaging graphics of DCS.

Catalyst TV Series Bumper

Catalyst is a television series covering the research done at Arizona State University, and the program needed an animated segue bumper. My goal for this project was to incorporate the series branding while adding more creative and playful tones to the animation. The final project was an 8-second bumper including Catalyst's logo, icons, and motto animated and published on YouTube.

UFC Lower Thirds Animation

For this project, the goal was to create lower thirds for a sports network that would present content in visually engaging animations. I chose to create lower thirds for the UFC, and I made quick, angular transitions to introduce content like fighter names and event bouts. The final output was a 14-second YouTube video demonstrating the transitions of 3 tiers of lower thirds.

Catch a Break: Music Video

Local Pittsburgh musician AVSTN reached out to me with the idea of a music video for his upcoming song, Catch A Break. The project entailed a fully animated 2D video that would tell an entertaining story to go along with the song. I created a simple narrative with cartoon designs and silly characters to create a fun and engaging animation, and the final product was a 3-minute long animated music video.

Movie Poster

In this project, I was tasked with creating a movie poster that would focus on masking and Photoshop skills. I created a fictional movie, photographed images to use in the composition, and designed the poster layout. My final outcome was a 27” x 40” design in Photoshop using several masking tools, blend modes, and photo editing.
